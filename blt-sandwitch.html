BLT Sandwitch

Delight in a swift and mouthwatering BLT Sandwich - crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, and juicy tomatoes unite for an effortlessly delicious treat. Enjoy the flavors!

Ingredients

tomato

cheese

bread

lettuce

butter

How to make an BLT Sandwitch

  1. 1 Cook bacon in frying pan or oven
  2. 2 Soften butter in microwave about 10 seconds
  3. 3 Brush butter on 2 sides of bread
  4. 4 Add 2 slices of cheese and filling ingredients - bacon, lettuce and tomato
  5. 5 Top with bread, buttered side up
  6. 6 Heat skillet or griddle, place sandwich buttered side down
  7. 7 Butter top piece of bread
  8. 8 Place saucepan on top of sandwich to add a little weight so the sandwiches ingredients combine and the cheese melts
  9. 9 Cook until brown, flip and cook until brown
  10. 10 Eat I guess
