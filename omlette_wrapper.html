Omlette Wrap

Whip up a tasty Omelette Wrap - a quick and delicious treat! Fluffy eggs with savory fillings make this recipe a breeze to prepare. Enjoy!

How to make an omlette wrapp

  1. 1 Start by cooking your bacon and veggies in a skillet the same size as the tortilla.
  2. 2 Spread in an even layer and pour in the eggs. Swirl the pan to redistribute the eggs in a round omelet shape about the same size as the tortilla.
  3. 3 Top the eggs with the tortilla on top and press the tortilla down â€“ the uncooked part of the eggs will help the tortilla stick to the eggs.
  4. 4 Run a spatula around the outside of the omelet and then quickly flip the omelet. Cook the tortilla side of the omelet wrap for one minute until the tortilla is browned and crisp.
  5. 5 Transfer the omelet to a plate, roll up, and place seam side down. Cut the wrap in half and serve with hot sauce or salsa if you like.
